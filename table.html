Hnafetafl


Goals

The defenders (Player One) win if the king (center) escapes to any of the corner tiles
The attackers (Player Two) win if they capture the king, by surrounding him on four sides.

Movement

Any piece may move any number of vacant spaces in any straight line.
No piece may ever pass over another piece in its path.
An enemy piece is captured and removed from the board if you move one of your pieces so that the enemy piece becomes surrounded on two opposite sides.
A piece may be moved in between two enemy pieces without being captured.

Turns after one player either kills their opponent's piece or has moved their character as far as they wanted
